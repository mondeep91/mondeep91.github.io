My research interests

Currently I am working on Electromagnetic Frequency Selective Surface applications. Here are some of my interests:

  • Space time modulated FSS
  • Polarization rotator
  • Microwave absorbers

About Me

Mondeep Saikia received the B. Tech degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from National Institute of Technology Silchar, India in 2013, M. Tech in Electrical Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur, India in 2016. He is currently working towards his Ph.D. degree under the supervision of Dr. Kumar Vaibhav Srivastava in Electrical Engineering at IIT Kanpur. He has published 12 peer reviewed papers in international journals and 16 conference papers. His current research interests includes microwave absorbers, Frequency selective surfaces, space time modulated materials and polarization rotators. He is also an active member of IEEE, IEEE AP society and IEEE MTT society.
